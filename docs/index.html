Data for Total Maximum Daily Loads and Escherichia coli trends in Texas freshwater streams

Description: This dataset includes E. coli bacteria measurements and associated flow data for 778 water quality monitoring stations in Texas. The data was used to evaluate trends in E. coli bacteria concentration and the effectiveness of TMDLs at reducing E. coli concentrations.
